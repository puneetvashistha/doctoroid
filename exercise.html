Yoga Sessions

Yoga can help with back pain, flexibility, stress reduction, mood and sleep and provides various health benefits. Here is some beginner yoga poses and help from Doctoroid.

Legs Up the Wall | Viparita Karani

Legs up the wall promotes relaxation while stretching your hamstrings and supporting your circulatory system.

Bridge Pose | Setu Bandha Sarvangasana

Bridge pose promotes core and glute strength and, in doing so, helps prevent back pain.

Chair Pose | Utkatasana

In this pose, you mimic sitting in a chair, as you hold a static squat with your feet together.
